\subsection{Detailed project status}

Our team is thrilled to share that we've made big steps forward in the building of our payload. The prototyping process has begun, which allows us to thoroughly test how each sensor works. Also, we've made different payload case designs to test how durable they are, and all of them have done great under tough conditions. Now that we have chosen the key parts and materials, our attention turns to putting them together in the best way possible so that the payload design exceeds other designs. As we move forward, we highlight how important it is to keep testing and making changes. The final PCB assembly and testing phase is scheduled for the coming weeks, which will ensure that the payload's electronics work at their best.
